# Resources

Additional resources and materials to help you prepare for the HAGAR exam.

## Official Government Resources

- [Transport Canada Hang Glider Study Guide (TP 11408E)](https://tc.canada.ca/en/aviation/publications/study-reference-guide-air-law-procedures-class-e-airspace-tp-11408)
- [Transport Canada Category 4 Medical Declaration Form](https://wwwapps.tc.gc.ca/Corp-Serv-Gen/5/forms-formulaires/search/results?Keywords=&FormNumber=26-0297)
- [Transport Canada Canada's Airspace Poster (TP 6010)](https://www.tc.gc.ca/Publications/BIL/TP6010/PDF/HR/TP6010B.PDF)
- [Transport Canada Aeronautical Information Manual (TC AIM) (TP 14371E)](https://tc.canada.ca/en/aviation/publications/transport-canada-aeronautical-information-manual-tc-aim-tp-14371)
- [Canadian Aviation Regulations (CARs)](https://tc.canada.ca/en/corporate-services/acts-regulations/list-regulations/canadian-aviation-regulations-sor-96-433)
- [Transport Canada Pilot Decision-Making (TP 8940E)](https://tc.canada.ca/en/aviation/general-operating-flight-rules/better-pilot-decision-making)
- [Transport Canada Human Factors for Aviation](https://tc.canada.ca/en/aviation/publications/human-factors-brochure)
- [ISED RIC-21 Study Guide (ROC-A Exam)](https://ised-isde.canada.ca/site/spectrum-management-telecommunications/en/licences-and-certificates/radiocom-information-circulars-ric/ric-21-study-guide-restricted-operator-certificate-aeronautical-qualification-roc)
- [Transport Canada Exam Scheduling](https://examens-aviation-exams.tc.canada.ca/en/schedule/step1/) - Schedule HAGAR exam at TC offices (free)
- [Transport Canada Approved Invigilators](https://tc.canada.ca/en/aviation/licensing-pilots-personnel/flight-crew-licences-permits-ratings/how-book-flight-crew-exam#find_an_aei) - Private exam centers (prices vary)

## Navigation & Charts

- [NAV CANADA Online Store](https://products.navcanada.ca/shop-vfr/) - Official VNC charts (paid)
- [NAV CANADA Flight Planning Portal](https://spaces.navcanada.ca/workspace/flightplanning/) - Weather, NOTAMs, flight planning
- [NAV CANADA Weather & NOTAMs](https://plan.navcanada.ca/wxrecall/) - Current weather and NOTAMs
- [FltPlan.com](https://www.fltplan.com/) - Free VFR charts (create account, download Canadian sectionals)
- [Canadian Airspace Viewer](https://airspace.canadarasp.com/) - Interactive airspace map
