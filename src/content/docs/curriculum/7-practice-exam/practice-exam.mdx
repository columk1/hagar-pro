This practice exam is designed to mirror the official Transport Canada HAGAR written examination to test your readiness.

- Exam Format: 40 multiple-choice questions total.
- Question Breakdown: 33 theory questions across the core sections, plus 7 map-reading questions.
- Passing Target: 60% (24 correct answers).
- Time Limit: 2.5 hours.

For the most realistic test experience, we highly recommend using a physical paper copy of the Vancouver VNC along with your ruler and protractor. However, if you don't have your paper chart handy, don't worry—a built-in, zoomable digital VNC is provided within the test for you to pan around and find your answers.
